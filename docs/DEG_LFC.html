DOX TSS location only

I took the background of annotated genes (not expressed near genes, just nearest actual gene), I then filtered that list to only include peaks that span a TSS (distance to TSS = 0) I then added the assigned closest expressed near-gene table that I made using peaks to link. Next I made sure the peaks were unique and added in ATAC LFC for the treatment by 3 hours or 24 hours by peak. Last, I linked the LFC-RNA at 3 or 24 hours to the respective ATAC_LFC using neargene associated with that peak. This is all without limits of distance to neargene.
